Something about history of coffee in Romania

Coffee arrived in Romania at the beginning of the 20th century and was drunk more by aristocrats.

Specific to Romania is the "marghilomana" coffee that comes from the name of the politician Alexandru Marghiloman. He, on a hunt, wanted to drink coffee and because he did not have water, his coffee was prepared with cognac.

All the aristocrats of that period served in the cafes just so called: "marghilomana".
